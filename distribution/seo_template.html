The Problem With Cold Outreach

Most companies blast templates to random people. They pay $99/month for outreach tools, send 50 messages a day, and get 1 reply. That's a 2% response rate — if they're lucky.

The issue isn't the tool. It's the timing. You're interrupting people who weren't thinking about your solution.

How Intent Signals Work

Buyers leave signals everywhere. The trick is catching them before competitors do.

Reddit Purchase Intent

High-intent signal:"Looking for alternatives to [Tool] — budget $800/mo. Current tool sends spam and costs too much."

This person is actively shopping. They have budget, needs, and frustration. That's a 9.4/10 signal.

LinkedIn Complaint Threads

High-intent signal:"Our $400/mo outreach tool gets 2% reply rate. Thinking of cancelling. Better options for B2B agencies?"

They're evaluating alternatives right now. Unhappy, spending money, asking for advice. That's a 9.1/10.

The Signal Scoring System

  • Buying language — "looking for", "alternatives", "budget"
  • Urgency — "right now", "this week", "evaluating"
  • Budget signals — specific dollar amounts
  • Problem severity — emotional frustration language
  • Competitor mentions — naming unhappy tools
