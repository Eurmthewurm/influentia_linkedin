Hey PH! I'm Ermo, founder of Influentia.

We built this after spending 3 months on cold outreach. Results: 847 connection requests, 3 replies, 0 calls, $280/mo wasted on tools.

Then we changed our entire approach: instead of sending first, we started listening.

We monitored Reddit and LinkedIn for people actively complaining about problems we solve. When someone said "I need X" and we replied "Here's X" — the reply rate went from 0.8% to 34%. 42x better.

Influentia automates this. It finds buyer signals, scores them by intent level, and drafts contextual responses. Not templates — real messages grounded in what the buyer actually said.

Current pricing: $97/mo with a 14-day trial. We're keeping it affordable because we built this for small agencies and indie SaaS teams like ours.

Happy to answer any questions about how the signal scoring works, or share more data from our early tests!
