Influentia vs Expandi

Both tools automate LinkedIn outreach. The difference is where they run, how they write messages, and how much LinkedIn notices. Here's what actually matters when you're choosing between them.

TL;DR
  • Expandi runs in the cloud — on their IP, not yours. LinkedIn flags cloud-originated actions far more often than browser sessions from your own machine.
  • Influentia runs locally on your computer. Your IP, your browser profile, your session. Indistinguishable from you doing it manually.
  • Expandi sends template sequences. Influentia reads each prospect's recent LinkedIn posts before writing the message — no two messages are the same.
  • Expandi costs $99/month. Influentia is $97/month.

The IP address problem

Expandi runs in the cloud, which means every action — profile views, connection requests, messages — comes from a datacenter IP. LinkedIn's risk engine is trained to detect exactly this. Thousands of Expandi users share the same IP ranges, and LinkedIn knows it.

Influentia runs on your machine. The browser that opens is a real Chromium instance with your saved session and your home or office IP. To LinkedIn, it looks identical to you opening your laptop and doing outreach manually. That's not a marketing claim — it's just how the technology works.

The tradeoff: your computer needs to be on for Influentia to run. If you want true 24/7 automation without thinking about it, a cloud tool gives you that. But if you'd rather not risk your LinkedIn account, local wins.

Templates vs actual messages

Expandi's personalisation is {{firstName}}, {{company}}, and custom variables you populate manually. The structure and angle of every message is the same — only the nouns change. Prospects have seen these. They recognise them.

Before Influentia writes a message, it visits the prospect's recent-activity page and reads what they've actually been posting about. If they wrote about a hiring challenge last week, that's the hook. If they shared a milestone, that's the observation. The message is written fresh each time — there's no template underneath it.

This isn't a small difference. Reply rates on activity-aware messages are meaningfully higher because the recipient can tell someone actually looked at their profile.

Price

Expandi is $99/month. Influentia is $97/month — same ballpark, fundamentally different architecture. Both include connection requests, follow-ups, and inbox management. There's no per-seat upsell on Influentia and no feature gating — everything is in the base plan.

Bottom line

Expandi is a cloud tool. Influentia runs on your machine. That single difference affects everything: your account safety, how your messages read, and whether prospects can tell it's automation. If you're a founder who values their LinkedIn account and wants replies — not just sends — Influentia is the clear choice.
