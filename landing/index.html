FAQ

Questions?

LinkedIn's ToS discourages automation. But there's a spectrum. Outreach Pilot runs on your machine, respects rate limits, personalises every message, and pauses on warnings — the opposite of the cloud scrapers that get accounts banned. Most users run it without issues. That said, use it responsibly, start slow, and test carefully. We're not liable for account flags, but we design for account health.
14-day free trial with full access. A card is required upfront — you won't be charged until day 15. After that, €29/month. Cancel anytime from your account portal with one click. No questions, no fees.
You bring your own Claude API key (from Anthropic) and Brave Search key (free up to 2,000 searches/month). You pay those providers directly — we never touch your usage or mark up the cost. For typical usage, Claude costs around €2–5/month. Brave is free for most users.
Your leads, messages, and settings live on your computer. They stay there forever — we never had them. We delete your email and billing record from our servers. The only data we keep is what's required by law for tax purposes.
The app is designed to avoid flags: human-like delays, CAPTCHA detection, auto-pause on warnings. But LinkedIn's moderation isn't perfect. If your account gets a warning, resolve it directly with LinkedIn, then click Resume in the dashboard. We recommend starting with 5–10 requests/day and ramping up slowly.
