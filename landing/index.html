FAQ

Common questions, answered honestly.

Is LinkedIn automation safe to use?
LinkedIn's Terms of Service discourage automation. Influentia minimises risk by running from your own IP (not a shared cloud server), using conservative daily caps (15 connections, 20 messages, 5 comments/day), inserting human-like delays, and pausing on any CAPTCHA. Cloud tools like Expandi and Phantombuster have higher ban rates because hundreds of users share the same IPs — making them trivial for LinkedIn to detect.
What makes Influentia different from Expandi or Phantombuster?
Influentia runs entirely on your own computer. Expandi, Phantombuster, and similar tools run on shared cloud servers — your actions share an IP with hundreds of other users. Influentia also reads each prospect's actual LinkedIn posts before writing a message, rather than using templates with variable substitution. No other tool combines local-first execution with AI that reads prospect activity.
Do I need LinkedIn Sales Navigator?
No. Influentia finds LinkedIn profiles by searching the public web using your ICP filters — job titles, industries, locations, keywords. No Sales Navigator or LinkedIn Premium required.
Does it require API keys?
No. AI (Claude) and web search are bundled with the subscription. You only need your LinkedIn credentials to get started.
Where does my data go?
Your lead database, conversations, and LinkedIn session live entirely on your computer. Influentia's servers only store your email, license key, and Stripe subscription metadata. Nothing else leaves your machine.
Can it find leads on Reddit?
Yes. Influentia scans Reddit daily for posts expressing buying intent for your specific offering. AI scores each post 1–10. High-scoring posts (8+) queue for autopilot — you review and approve before anything posts. Up to 3 Reddit replies per night.
What are the daily limits?
LinkedIn: 15 connection requests, 20 messages, 5 comments per day. Reddit: up to 3 autopilot replies per night (8+ signals only). These caps are conservative by design — your LinkedIn account is your most valuable sales asset, not a throwaway.
