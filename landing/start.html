Setup Guide

Get Influentia running in 10 minutes. No API keys needed — everything is bundled.

1 Download & Install

Download the app and unzip it to your Desktop:

⬇ Download Influentia.zip
After downloading
  • Step 1: Double-click influentia.zip → a folder named influentia appears. Open it.
  • Step 2: Find Install.command inside. Right-click it → click Open → click Open again when Mac warns you.
  • Step 3: A black Terminal window opens. Click inside it, then type your Mac login password. You won't see dots or letters while typing — this is Mac's security feature. Press Enter.
  • Step 4: Watch the text scroll. When it stops and you see a line like $ or % at the bottom, the install is done. Close that Terminal window.
  • Step 5: Influentia opens in your browser at http://localhost:5555. You're in.
⚠️ Important:
• Do NOT open Terminal.app yourself and type commands.
• Right-click → Open on Install.command (not double-click the first time — Mac blocks it).
• The password field is blank while you type — type it anyway and press Enter.

💡 The whole install takes about 2–5 minutes. If you run into issues, email support@influentia.io.

2 Activate Your License

When Influentia opens for the first time, you'll see a license entry screen:

  • Paste your license key (the one from your email or the success page)
  • Click Activate
  • Influentia verifies your license and unlocks the dashboard

3 Connect LinkedIn

Influentia needs access to your LinkedIn account to scan for buyer signals and send messages:

  • In the wizard, click "Connect LinkedIn"
  • A browser window opens to LinkedIn's login page
  • Log in normally (just like you would in Safari/Chrome)
  • Once you see your LinkedIn feed, the connection is complete
  • Close that browser window and return to Influentia
🔒 Privacy note: Your LinkedIn session is stored locally on your computer. Influentia never sees your password. LinkedIn sees actions coming from your IP, from your browser profile — indistinguishable from you doing it manually.

4 Define Your ICP

Tell Influentia who you're looking for. Be specific:

  • What you sell: "I help B2B SaaS founders generate leads from LinkedIn"
  • Who you help: "Founders at companies with 1-20 employees, $1k-10k MRR"
  • Their pain: "Struggling with lead generation, no SDR team, burning out on outreach"

5 Run Your First Scan

Click "Scan Now" in the dashboard. Influentia will:

  • Scan 12+ subreddits and LinkedIn comment threads for buyer-intent signals
  • AI-scores each signal 0–10 for relevance to your ICP
  • Drafts personalized replies for signals scored 8+
  • Queues drafts in your approval inbox
  • First scan takes about 5 minutes

✓ Review & Send

Your first drafts are waiting in the approval queue:

  • Read each draft — edit anything that doesn't sound like you
  • Click "Approve & Send" to send, or "Skip" to pass
  • LinkedIn messages send from your account, on your IP, with human-like delays
  • Reddit replies can auto-post overnight (optional, capped at 3/night)
What to expect
  • Day 1-3: Influentia is learning your voice. Review every draft carefully and edit freely.
  • Day 4-7: Drafts start sounding more like you. You'll spend less time editing.
  • Week 2+: Replies come back. Expect 2-5 conversations per week with a well-defined ICP.
