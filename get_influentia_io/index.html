Influentia

Local-first LinkedIn + Reddit outreach.
Runs on your machine. Your data never leaves.

macOS
$ curl -fsSL https://get.influentia.io/install.sh | bash

Paste this in Terminal (press Enter).
Takes about 2 minutes. Your browser will open automatically.

What happens

1
Downloads Influentia + creates an isolated Python environment
2
Installs Chromium (the browser Influentia uses to reach LinkedIn)
3
Starts the server and opens the setup wizard in your browser
4
Enter your license key → connect LinkedIn → you're live
